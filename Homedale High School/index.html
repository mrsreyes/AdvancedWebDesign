Homedale girls basketball team ends Parma's three-year league unbeaten.

22 Jan 2019, Brandon Walton

HOMEDALE — Cam Long jested to Parma coach Michael Calkins before the game his team was going to end the streak. It turned out the Homedale girls basketball coach wasn’t playing around. Homedale stunned No. 2 Parma 44-42 to end the Panthers’ 24-game 3A Snake River Valley Conference winning streak Tuesday night. It was the Panthers’ first loss against a conference opponent since a 68-59 defeat to Emmett in the 3A District III Tournament third-place game on Feb. 11, 2016. “This is by far our biggest win not only of the season, but in quite some time,” Long said. “I mean no one was talking about Homedale, and why should they? Our record is what it is, so I’m just so proud of them.” (...)
